For & By Art Gallery

Created by us. Curated for all.

For & By Art Gallery is dedicated to showcasing the work of Black American artists whose practices are rooted in community, memory, and innovation. This collection is created for the culture, by the culture—a living archive of Black expression across mediums and generations.

Come & be inspired

Tuesdays: Sip & Study Join us for an intimate evening of artistic exchange. Each week, a local artist leads a hands-on session exploring a new technique—from collage to printmaking—paired with curated wines and artisanal cheeses. No experience necessary, just curiosity.

Wednesdays: Process in Progress Experience the creative act in real time. Watch a featured artist at work in our open studio space while enjoying seasonal hors d’oeuvres. These sessions offer a rare glimpse into the evolving gestures behind finished pieces.

Founders: Alton & Renee Marshall

Alton and Renee Marshall opened the For & By Art Gallery with a singular vision: to create a space where Black art is not only seen, but deeply felt. Lifelong advocates for cultural equity and creative expression, the Marshalls have cultivated a gallery that honors legacy while nurturing the next generation of artists. Their presence is felt in every corner—from the curated walls to the welcoming energy that greets each visitor.

For & By Art Gallery
